英语补习计划课后练习

今天的课上了“现在、过去和将来进行时”

答案如下：

一、选择题

1.a 2.a 3.c

二、填空题

1.am writing 2.were playing 3.will be studying

三、改错题

1. He was playing football yesterday.

2. They will be playing a game tomorrow.

3. I was reading a book last night.

四、翻译题

1. I am doing my homework.

2. At this time yesterday, they were playing basketball.

3. Tomorrow at 3 PM, she will be studying for her exam.
